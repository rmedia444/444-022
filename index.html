Fuel and Lubricant Sales

At PetroChina CCCC Oil Products Sales Co., Ltd., we specialize in the sales of a wide range of fuels and lubricants. Our product offerings include gasoline, diesel (closed cup flash point ≤ 60°C), kerosene, and naphtha, all operated without storage facilities, ensuring compliance with safety regulations. Additionally, we provide high-quality lubricants and petroleum asphalt, catering to various industrial and automotive needs. Our No. 7 fuel oil and an array of chemical products (excluding hazardous chemicals and Class I precursor chemicals) are designed to meet the specific demands of our clients. We are committed to delivering premium fuel and lubricant solutions, backed by our hazardous chemicals business license valid until July 19, 2024.
